The Problem

Your phone is a slot machine
you never signed up for.

  • Notification fatigue is real

    The average professional receives 63+ notifications per day across Slack, email, text, and phone. Each one demands a decision: is this urgent or noise?

  • Unplugging is a luxury you cannot afford

    You want to be present at dinner, focused on deep work, or simply away from screens. But if your boss sends an urgent Slack, you need to know about it.

  • All-or-nothing tools fail you

    Do Not Disturb blocks everything. Smart watches forward everything. Manual rules break the moment someone new becomes important. There is no middle ground.

The Solution

An AI that knows
what actually matters to you.

SymPhoney connects to your email, Slack, text messages, and phone. It watches how you communicate: who you reply to fastest, which threads trigger immediate action, which senders you ignore.

Within days, it builds a priority model unique to you. Then it takes over as your intelligent filter, surfacing only the notifications that genuinely need your attention.

Core Features

Intelligence that gets
sharper every day.

Four capabilities that transform how you relate to your devices.

AI Priority Scoring

Machine learning analyzes your reply speed, frequency, and interaction patterns across every channel. It knows your VP of finance is urgent before you write a single rule.

Universal Inbox Integration

Slack, Gmail, Outlook, SMS, phone calls, and more. One secure hub sees everything so nothing slips through the cracks, regardless of which platform it comes from.

Smart Digest Mode

Low-priority notifications are batched into a clean daily or hourly summary. You stay informed without the constant interruption. Read it on your schedule.

Quick-Reply Relay

Respond to any Slack, email, or text from a simple SMS or smartwatch tap. Canned replies or short messages get routed back to the right platform automatically.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Launch with a browser extension and mobile app targeting remote workers and tech professionals on Product Hunt and Hacker News. Partner with corporate wellness programs to offer team deployments. Build organic traffic through content marketing around digital wellness and deep work productivity.

Competitive Advantage

Unlike basic Do Not Disturb modes or manual notification rules, SymPhoney builds a living model of what matters to each user. It learns continuously without requiring manual configuration, and it works across every communication platform rather than being locked to one ecosystem.

Year One Milestones

  • Launch beta with Slack, Gmail, and SMS integration; acquire 5,000 beta users within 3 months
  • Reach 1,000 paying Pro subscribers and secure 10 team-tier company accounts by month 8
  • Achieve 85% accuracy on priority scoring and release v2 with smartwatch relay and quick-reply features by month 12

Key Risks

  • Users may be uncomfortable granting broad access to all their communication channels, creating a significant privacy and trust barrier
  • Platform API restrictions from Slack, Gmail, or Apple could limit integration depth or break functionality without warning
  • Large incumbents like Apple, Google, or Microsoft could build similar AI notification filtering directly into their operating systems

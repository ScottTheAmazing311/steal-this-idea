Charity Buck

Slide your giving. Change the world.

A subscription-based charitable giving platform that lets you set a monthly donation budget, then seamlessly slide your pooled funds between vetted charities, emergency causes, and community projects with a single tap.

Start Giving Today See How It Works
The Problem

Giving Should Be Simple. It's Not.

Most people want to donate, but the current system makes it hard to know where your money goes, and even harder to change your mind.

Decision Paralysis

Thousands of organizations compete for your attention, leading to guilt-driven giving or, more often, no giving at all.

Zero Transparency

Unclear overhead rates make it nearly impossible to know what percentage of your dollar actually reaches the people who need it.

Rigid Donations

When a new crisis hits or a cause catches your eye, there is no easy way to redirect where your money goes without starting from scratch.

The Solution

Pool, Slide, and Give. In Seconds.

Charity Buck makes generosity feel as natural as swiping through your favorite app.

Frictionless, informed generosity

Charity Buck pools your daily micro-donations into a monthly fund, then lets you slide that money between curated, transparency-rated charities in seconds. When a disaster strikes or a cause catches your eye, one tap redirects your funds.

Key Features

Everything You Need to Give Better

Tools designed for a generation that demands transparency, flexibility, and real impact.

Slide-to-Give Allocation

Redirect your pooled donations between charities with one intuitive swipe. Your money, your call, anytime you want.

Transparency Scores

See exactly what percentage of each dollar reaches the cause. Every charity rated on overhead, impact, and accountability.

Smart Cause Groups

Automatically distribute your funds across multiple vetted organizations in a single category, like climate, education, or hunger relief.

Ambassador Program

Consistent donors earn the ability to nominate people in crisis for emergency grants, turning your generosity into direct community action.

Business Plan

How Charity Buck Becomes a Business

A clear roadmap from launch to scale, with realistic costs and transparent strategy.

Revenue Model

Small monthly platform fee ($1.99/month) plus optional premium tiers for corporate matching dashboards and ambassador-level features. Additional revenue from nonprofit partnerships who pay for featured placement and analytics on donor engagement.

Go-to-Market

Launch with a mobile-first app targeting socially conscious young professionals through Instagram and TikTok campaigns. Partner with 50 highly rated charities at launch for curated cause groups. Free tier for the first 3 months and referral bonuses to drive viral adoption.

Competitive Advantage

Unlike GoFundMe or direct charity websites, Charity Buck combines a subscription micro-donation model with real-time fund redirection, built-in transparency scoring, and a community emergency fund. The slide-to-give UX creates switching costs that grow with each month of giving history.

Startup Cost

$150K - $400K

Estimated range to reach a fully functional MVP with iOS and Android apps, charity onboarding pipeline, and initial marketing spend.

Key Risks

  • Regulatory complexity around holding and distributing charitable funds across state lines and maintaining nonprofit tax-deductible status
  • Building and maintaining accurate, up-to-date transparency scores for thousands of charities requires significant data infrastructure and editorial oversight
  • User acquisition cost may be high in a space where people are inherently cautious about new financial platforms handling their donations

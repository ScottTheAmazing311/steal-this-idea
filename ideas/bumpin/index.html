The Problem

Podcasting Is Still a One-Way Street

Listeners are full of reactions, stories, and expertise that match exactly what they hear. But there is no way to contribute those voices back into the show.

The Moment Passes

You hear something that sparks a story. By the time you tweet or email, the moment is gone and no one cares.

Text Cannot Capture It

280 characters on Twitter or a long email do not carry the energy, emotion, or context of hearing someone tell their story out loud.

No Connection Point

Podcasters want listener engagement, but voicemail lines and email inboxes are disconnected from the listening experience.

The Solution

Turn Listeners Into Participants

Bumpin connects listener voices directly to the moments that spark them. Simple for listeners, powerful for producers.

Features

Built for Podcasters Who Want More

Every feature is designed to make listener engagement feel natural, not forced.

One-Tap Recording

Tap once to start recording your reaction at any timestamp. Your audio is automatically tagged to the exact moment in the episode that sparked it.

Producer Dashboard

Review incoming bump-ins, trim audio clips, approve the best ones, and place them exactly where they fit. Full control stays with the creator.

Dynamic Audio Insertion

Approved clips are seamlessly woven into episodes without re-uploading. The episode evolves as more listeners share their stories.

Engagement Analytics

See which moments spark the most listener reactions. Understand your audience at a deeper level and shape future content around what resonates.

Business Plan

The Path to Launch

A clear roadmap from concept to revenue, built on real market dynamics and a proven SaaS model.

Revenue Model

Freemium SaaS pricing for podcast creators. Free tier with limited bump-ins per episode. Pro tier at $12/month for unlimited bump-ins, analytics, and moderation tools. Network tier at $49/month for multi-show management. Additional revenue from a premium listener app.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Launch with 50 beta podcasters in interview and commentary genres where listener stories add the most value. Offer a free tier to build adoption, then convert power users to Pro. Partner with podcast hosting platforms for native integration and broader reach.

Competitive Advantage

Unlike SoundCloud comments (text-only, music-focused) or standard voicemail lines, Bumpin is purpose-built for podcasts with timestamp-aware audio contributions and producer-controlled dynamic insertion. This creates a flywheel: more engagement leads to better episodes, which attracts more listeners.

Key Risks

  • Moderation burden on producers could slow adoption if the approval workflow is not frictionless
  • Audio quality inconsistency from listener-recorded clips may degrade the listening experience
  • Podcast hosting platforms could build similar features natively, reducing the need for a third-party tool

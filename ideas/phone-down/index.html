The Solution

Turn focus into a game you actually want to play.

Phone Down transforms staying off your phone from a chore into a challenge. Place your phone on the smart charging pad, watch the points climb, and compete with friends to see who can stay focused the longest.

1

Place your phone on the pad

The LED ring glows green. Points start accumulating at one per second, and your charging pad doubles as a wireless charger.

2

Build your streak

The longer your phone stays down, the faster you earn. After 30 minutes, you hit 3x speed. Your streak is your currency.

3

Compete and earn rewards

Climb the leaderboard, challenge friends to focus duels, and spend your points on avatar cosmetics or real brand-sponsored rewards.

Features

Everything you need to reclaim your focus.

Smart LED Streak Indicators

The charging pad glows green while you earn points and shifts to red when you pick up your phone. A gentle visual nudge that keeps you honest.

Accelerating Point System

Earn faster the longer your phone stays on the pad. Start at 1x and ramp up to 3x after 30 minutes. Pick up your phone and the multiplier resets.

Power Hour Mode

Schedule a deep focus window and earn 2x or 3x multiplied points. Perfect for hitting flow state during your most important work blocks.

Social Leaderboards and Avatar Store

Challenge friends, climb the leaderboard, and spend earned points on cosmetic upgrades for your avatar. Pirate hats, trails, and more.

Business Plan

The roadmap to making this real.

Revenue Model

Hardware sales of the wireless charging pad at $49 to $79 per unit. A freemium app with a premium tier at $4.99 per month for advanced analytics and custom power hours. Sponsored rewards from brands who pay to place coupons and offers in the reward store.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Launch on Kickstarter with a compelling video showing the LED charging pad in action. Seed units to productivity YouTubers and TikTok creators who already discuss focus and screen time. Partner with coworking spaces for bulk orders and sponsored challenges.

Competitive Advantage

Unlike punitive phone safes or basic screen time trackers, Phone Down uses positive reinforcement, social competition, and tangible rewards to make staying off your phone genuinely fun. The hardware plus software combination creates a physical ritual that pure software cannot replicate.

Year One Milestones

  • Launch Kickstarter and sell 10,000 units in the first 90 days
  • Ship v1 hardware and app, achieving 5,000 daily active users with 60%+ weekly retention
  • Sign 5 brand sponsors and generate first $50K in sponsorship revenue

Startup Cost and Key Risks

$150K to $400K

Covers hardware prototyping, mold tooling, app development, and initial inventory run.

  • Users may game the system by placing their phone on the pad without genuinely disengaging (e.g., using a tablet instead)
  • Hardware sourcing and manufacturing adds complexity and capital requirements compared to a pure software play
  • Apple and Android could integrate similar focus-reward features natively, reducing the need for a third-party product

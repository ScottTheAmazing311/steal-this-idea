RETURN POST

A one-stop retail return center where you drop off items from multiple stores, get gift cards back, and leave in under three minutes. No more piling up returns in the back of your car for months.

HOW IT WORKS
Returns processed in under 3 minutes

THE PROBLEM

RETURNS ARE BROKEN

Returning purchases is one of the most dreaded errands in modern life. Most people would rather keep an unwanted item than deal with the hassle.

01

The pile-up problem

Returns accumulate in your car, your closet, your counter. Items from five different stores mean five different trips. Most never get returned at all.

02

Time is the real cost

Driving to multiple stores, waiting in long customer service lines, arguing about receipts and credit cards. A simple return can eat up an entire afternoon.

03

The shame factor

Nobody enjoys standing at a return counter explaining why they do not want something. The social friction alone keeps millions of dollars in unwanted goods sitting in homes.

THE SOLUTION

DROP IT. DONE.

Return Post partners with major retailers to accept returns for multiple brands under one roof. Walk in, drop off, walk out.

Return Post is a standalone retail location where customers bring back items from any partner store. You walk in, hand over your returns, and receive gift cards to the original retailer. The entire process takes less than three minutes.

Stores love it because gift cards keep money in their ecosystem, and roughly one-third of gift card balances go unspent. Customers love it because they reclaim their time and their trunk space.

One location for returns from 10+ major retailers
3-minute guaranteed turnaround time
Gift card refunds deposited to your Return Post profile
No receipts or credit cards needed after sign-up

KEY FEATURES

BUILT FOR SPEED AND SIMPLICITY

3-MINUTE RETURNS

Walk in, drop off your items, and leave. Every Return Post employee is trained and measured on one metric: how fast they process your return. No waiting, no haggling.

MULTI-BRAND HUB

Target, Walmart, Nordstrom, Best Buy, and more. One location accepts returns from all partner retailers. Stop driving to five stores when you can visit one.

INSTANT GIFT CARDS

Refunds are issued as digital gift cards to the original retailer. They are deposited straight into your Return Post profile, ready to spend whenever you want.

FRICTIONLESS PROFILES

Sign up once with your name and payment info. After that, every return is a quick drop-off. No digging for receipts, no swiping credit cards, no explaining yourself.

MARKET OPPORTUNITY

$800 BILLION IN ANNUAL RETURNS

A MASSIVE, UNDERSERVED MARKET

US retail returns total over $800 billion every year. Amazon has already proven that third-party return hubs work through partnerships with Kohl's and UPS. But nobody has built a brand-agnostic, multi-retailer return center focused purely on speed and convenience.

The gift card model is a proven winner for retailers. Roughly one-third of gift card value goes unspent, creating pure profit. Return Post turns a pain point into a revenue stream for everyone involved.

BUSINESS PLAN

FROM CONCEPT TO LAUNCH

REVENUE MODEL

Return Post takes a small percentage (2 to 5%) of each return transaction value. Additional revenue from brand partnership fees, where retailers pay for placement and customer data insights. Volume-based model that scales with transactions per location.

GO-TO-MARKET STRATEGY

Open a pilot location in a high-traffic suburban strip mall. Partner with 5 to 10 major retailers (Target, Walmart, Nordstrom, Best Buy) to prove the concept. Use speed and convenience as the core marketing message. Expand through franchise or company-owned locations in top metro areas.

COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE

Amazon has proven the model works through Kohl's partnerships, but nobody has built a brand-agnostic, multi-retailer return hub. Return Post removes friction for consumers while offering retailers a way to keep revenue in-house through gift cards rather than losing it to cash refunds.

STARTUP COST ESTIMATE

Estimated $150,000 to $350,000 to reach MVP. Covers first retail lease, buildout, POS system, inventory logistics software, initial staffing, brand partnership negotiations, and marketing for the pilot launch.

YEAR ONE MILESTONES

  • Secure partnerships with at least 5 major retail brands for pilot program
  • Open first Return Post location and process 1,000 returns in first month
  • Achieve 90%+ customer satisfaction rating and sub-3-minute average return time

KEY RISKS

  • Major retailers may refuse to partner or create their own consolidated return solutions
  • Customers who expect cash back may resist the gift-card-only return model
  • Logistics of processing and routing returns to multiple retailers could be operationally complex

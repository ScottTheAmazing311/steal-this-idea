BUSINESS PLAN

The Blueprint

REVENUE MODEL

Free for consumers. Revenue comes from affiliate commissions paid by credit card issuers for each approved referral ($50 to $200+ per application). The service creates a frictionless funnel from personalized recommendation to affiliate click-through.

GO-TO-MARKET STRATEGY

Launch with social ads showing a simple hook: "You left $600 on the table last year." Drive users to a free sign-up where they link their bank account and instantly see potential savings. Viral sharing is built in as users share their savings gap on social media.

COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE

Unlike The Points Guy or NerdWallet, which offer static editorial recommendations, CardShark delivers dynamic, personalized advice based on actual transaction data. The longer a user stays connected, the more accurate recommendations become, creating a sticky data moat.

YEAR ONE MILESTONES

  • Launch MVP with Plaid integration and top 100 credit card database
  • Acquire 50,000 users and generate 10,000 affiliate referrals
  • Secure partnerships with 3+ major credit card issuers for premium placement

KEY RISKS

  • Consumer trust and willingness to share bank data with a new platform
  • Credit card companies changing affiliate commission structures or terms
  • Competition from established players like NerdWallet or Mint adding similar features

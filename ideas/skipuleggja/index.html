Design Your Organizer
The Problem

Your counter is a warzone. Your drawers are worse.

Every morning starts the same way: rummaging through a mess of bottles, brushes, and tubes scattered across your bathroom counter. You have tried the baskets, the trays, the acrylic organizers from Amazon. Nothing fits right.

You are forced into an impossible choice: leave everything out in a cluttered heap, or hide it all in drawers where the chaos just moves out of sight.

  • Generic organizers never fit your specific products
  • Drawers become junk drawers within days
  • Couples sharing a space doubles the problem
  • Off-the-shelf solutions look cheap and feel disposable
The Solution

Designed around you. Built to last.

Skipuleggja flips the script on organization. Instead of forcing your belongings into generic compartments, you tell us what you own. Our online configurator lets you select your exact products, and we create a precision 3D-printed insert molded to hold every single item perfectly.

That custom insert sits inside a beautiful handcrafted frame made from real stained wood. Oak, walnut, or bamboo. It looks stunning on your counter. It fits perfectly in your drawer. Every item has its place, and your space finally feels like your own.

Business Plan

The blueprint for building Skipuleggja

A clear-eyed look at how this concept becomes a company.

Revenue Model

Direct-to-consumer e-commerce. Countertop organizers priced at $79 to $149, drawer inserts at $49 to $99, and kitchen sets at $99 to $179. Premium frame upgrades (walnut, brass, matte black steel) drive higher average order values. A subscription refill program lets customers swap inserts as their product collection changes.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Launch with a focused Instagram and TikTok campaign built around dramatic before/after bathroom transformations. Partner with home organization influencers and interior design creators. Start with bathroom organizers as the hero product, then expand into kitchen drawers and desk setups based on customer demand.

Competitive Advantage

Every Skipuleggja unit is unique to the customer. Mass-customization via 3D printing combined with premium natural materials creates a product that no off-the-shelf competitor can match on fit or aesthetics. The configurator itself becomes a moat: the more product data collected, the better the recommendations become.

Year One Milestones

  • Launch online configurator and ship first 1,000 custom organizers within 6 months
  • Achieve $500K in revenue and 30% repeat purchase rate by end of year one
  • Expand product line from bathroom to kitchen and desk organization categories

Key Risks

  • 3D printing at scale may create production bottlenecks and longer lead times than customers expect
  • Custom units are non-resalable, so returns could erode margins if sizing or satisfaction issues arise
  • Large competitors like IKEA could introduce similar modular systems at significantly lower price points

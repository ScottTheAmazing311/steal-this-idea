The Problem

Your front door is completely dumb.

It's the most important communication point of your home, and it can't say a single thing.

Doorbell wakes the baby

You finally got them down for a nap. The delivery driver doesn't know. Ring ring. Game over.

Missed delivery instructions

Your handwritten note blew away. The driver left your package in the rain. Again.

Invisible house numbers

Guests circle the block twice because they can't see your house number after dark.

No way to say "come around back"

You're in the backyard and can't hear the doorbell. Your guests stand there awkwardly for 10 minutes.

The Solution

Your door, now brilliant.

Marq-E is a weatherproof, Bluetooth-enabled e-ink display that turns your front door into a smart communication hub.

1

Mount it

Stick Marq-E to your door, window, or wall. Weatherproof and sleek, it looks like it belongs there.

2

Load your messages

Choose from presets or write your own. "Baby sleeping," delivery instructions, your house number, whatever you need.

3

Tap or say it

One tap in the app, or just say "Alexa, tell Marq-E I'm busy." The display updates instantly.

4

Auto-revert

Set a timer and Marq-E automatically switches back to your default display. Set it and forget it.

Key Features

Simple tech. Surprisingly powerful.

Everything you need to make your front door the smartest part of your home.

One-Tap Presets

Load your favorite messages and activate them with a single tap. Auto-revert timers mean you never forget to switch back to your default display.

Illuminated House Numbers

Your house number, beautifully lit and visible from the street at night. No more circling the block; guests and drivers find you instantly.

Voice Control

"Alexa, tell Marq-E I'm busy." Works with Alexa and Google Home so you can update your door without lifting a finger.

Weeks of Battery Life

E-ink technology means exceptional battery life and perfect readability in direct sunlight. Fully weatherproof: rain, snow, or shine.

Business Plan

How Marq-E becomes a business.

A clear path from concept to product to revenue.

Revenue Model

Hardware sales at $39–49 per unit with healthy margins on a simple e-ink + Bluetooth module. Optional premium app subscription at $2.99/month for scheduling, custom designs, and smart home integrations. The gift market is enormous; perfect for housewarmings, holidays, and new parents.

Go-to-Market

Launch on Kickstarter to validate demand and build community. Target the new-parent and new-homeowner gift market through social media. Partner with Ring/Nest communities and smart home influencers. Scale to retail placement at Target and Home Depot as an impulse-buy smart home accessory.

Competitive Advantage

Ring solved "who's at my door." Marq-E solves "what should my door tell them." At under $50, it's cheaper than a Ring and serves a complementary purpose. E-ink means exceptional battery life and daylight readability. The gift-ability factor creates organic word-of-mouth growth.

Startup Cost

$50,000 – $120,000

Covers hardware prototyping, app development, initial manufacturing run, and Kickstarter campaign production. Lean enough for angel funding or bootstrapping with a strong crowdfunding campaign.

Year One Milestones

  • Successfully fund Kickstarter campaign and ship first 5,000 units
  • Achieve 4.5+ star average rating to build word-of-mouth credibility
  • Secure retail placement in one major chain (Target, Home Depot, or Best Buy)

Key Risks

  • Battery life and weatherproofing must be bulletproof or returns will kill the brand
  • Smart home fatigue: consumers may see it as yet another gadget they don't need
  • Large players (Ring, Google) could easily add a display feature to existing doorbells

Business Plan

The playbook for growth.

Revenue Model

Freemium SaaS for restaurants. Basic listing and offer capability is free. Premium tiers ($49 to $199/month) unlock analytics dashboards, demographic insights, peak-time data, advanced promotion tools, and priority placement. Additional revenue from featured placements and loyalty program integrations.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Launch in 3 to 5 major tourist destinations (Rome, Tokyo, Cancun) by signing up restaurant clusters in high-traffic dining districts. Offer the app free to both sides initially to build network density. Partner with travel influencers and hotel concierge services to drive tourist downloads. Expand to U.S. foodie cities (NYC, LA, Austin) once the model is proven.

Competitive Advantage

Unlike Yelp or Google Maps, which show static reviews, Bon Appetit creates a live, two-way marketplace where restaurants actively compete for your visit with real-time incentives. Every session is different, offers are personalized, and restaurants can solve supply problems (cancellations, surplus inventory, slow nights) instantly.

Year One Milestones

  • Launch in 3 tourist-heavy cities with 500+ restaurant partners and 50,000 app downloads
  • Achieve 15% weekly active user rate with an average of 2+ dining sessions per active user per month
  • Convert 10% of free restaurant partners to premium paid tiers, reaching $25K monthly recurring revenue

Startup Cost Estimate

Key Risks

  • Classic chicken-and-egg marketplace problem: need enough restaurants to attract users and enough users to attract restaurants
  • Established players like Yelp, Google Maps, and OpenTable could replicate the real-time offer feature with their existing user bases
  • Restaurants may resist giving away free items or discounts if they do not see measurable ROI quickly enough

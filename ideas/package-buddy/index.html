Your porch, finally pirate-proof.

The Problem

1.7 Million Packages Stolen Every Single Day

Porch piracy is not a minor inconvenience. It is a multi-billion dollar crisis that gets worse every holiday season. Ring doorbells capture the footage, but they rarely stop the theft. Your package is gone, and all you have is a blurry video of someone in a hoodie.

Current solutions fall short. Porch lockers are bulky, expensive, and impossible for apartment dwellers. Amazon Key requires handing over access to your home. And food delivery? You are stuck hovering by the door for hours, unable to shower, start a movie, or relax.

The Solution

Mount It. Lock It. Forget It.

PackageBuddy is a premium, insulated delivery bag that hangs on any front door with a secure over-door hook. No drilling, no smart locks on your deadbolt, no permission from your landlord. Just hang it up and you are protected.

1
Hang it on your door. The universal hook fits any standard door. Takes under 30 seconds.
2
Share the code or tap. Tell your delivery driver the access code, or let them tap with NFC.
3
Get notified, grab it later. Your phone buzzes when a delivery arrives. Pick it up whenever you are ready.
Features

Designed for Real Life

Universal Door Mount

Fits any standard door with a sturdy over-door hook system. No drilling, no tools, no landlord approval. Set up in under 30 seconds and take it with you when you move.

Smart NFC Lock + App

Delivery drivers tap their phone to unlock. You get an instant push notification when your delivery is secured inside. Grant and revoke access right from the PackageBuddy app.

Insulated Lining

A removable thermal liner keeps food hot and groceries cold for up to two hours. Your burrito stays warm, your ice cream stays frozen. Swap liners for easy cleaning.

Low-Profile Design

Available in matte black, slate gray, and espresso brown. Looks like it belongs on your door, not like an afterthought. Premium materials that are weather resistant and durable.

Market Opportunity

A $4 Billion Problem Waiting to Be Solved

The U.S. processes over 20 billion parcels every year, and that number grows with each passing quarter. Meanwhile, 36% of Americans have experienced package theft. The pain is universal, but the solutions are not.

PackageBuddy targets the massive underserved segment: renters, apartment dwellers, townhome owners, and anyone in an HOA-restricted neighborhood. These people cannot install porch lockers or smart door locks, but they still deserve secure deliveries.

44 million renter households in the U.S.
20+ billion parcels delivered annually
$300B global food delivery market
Business Plan

The Roadmap to Launch

Revenue Model

Direct-to-consumer e-commerce with three product tiers. Additional recurring revenue from replacement insulated liners, seasonal designs, and a premium app subscription for multi-device households.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Launch on Kickstarter to validate demand and generate buzz with a target of $150K. Follow up with a Shopify DTC storefront, targeted social ads on Instagram and TikTok focused on apartment dwellers and frequent online shoppers. Partner with apartment review sites and property management newsletters for organic acquisition.

Competitive Advantage

PackageBuddy is the only delivery security product that works for both packages and food, requires zero installation, and fits apartments, townhomes, and HOA-restricted homes. The combination of universal compatibility, insulated lining, and smart lock technology creates a defensible position that porch locker competitors cannot match.

Year One Milestones

  • Raise $150K on Kickstarter and ship first 3,000 units within 6 months
  • Achieve $500K in DTC revenue and 10,000 units sold by end of year one
  • Secure pilot partnerships with 3 major apartment management companies

Key Risks

  • Consumer adoption requires behavior change from both buyers and delivery drivers
  • Potential for copycats from established brands like Ring or Amazon entering the category
  • Weight limits may frustrate customers who expect it to hold very large or heavy packages

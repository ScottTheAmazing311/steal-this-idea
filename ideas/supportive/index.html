The Problem

Your money speaks louder than your voice. But do you know what it is saying?

Consumers increasingly want to support brands that align with their values, whether that means environmental conservation, cruelty-free products, or charitable giving. But finding out which brands actually back these causes takes hours of scattered research across dozens of websites.

Gift givers face the same frustration. You want a present that carries deeper meaning, but you have no idea which companies donate to causes your friend cares about. And if someone in your life is vegan, keto, or non-GMO, finding compliant products feels like detective work.

The Solution

One search. Every value. Complete clarity.

Supportive aggregates brand data on causes, certifications, donations, and lifestyle compatibility into a single searchable platform. Look up any brand. Filter by what matters to you. Discover companies you never knew existed.

  • Search any brand to see its causes, donations, and certifications
  • Filter products by environmental, social, or lifestyle values
  • Find gifts that carry real meaning for the people you love
  • Navigate dietary and lifestyle needs with confidence
Key Features

Built for conscious consumers

Every feature is designed to close the gap between what you believe and where you spend.

Brand Values Lookup

Search any brand to instantly see its supported causes, certifications, and charitable giving history. Know exactly where your money goes before you spend it.

Cause-Based Discovery

Filter products across every category by the movements and values you care about most. From environmental conservation to fair labor practices, find brands that walk the talk.

Gift Finder

Shop by recipient values and interests, with price filters and curated gift guides for every occasion. Turn every gift into something that carries real meaning beyond the wrapping.

Lifestyle Compatibility

Find products that match dietary and lifestyle needs like vegan, keto, non-GMO, or cruelty-free. No more guessing, no more label hunting, just quick, confident choices.

Market Opportunity

A massive and growing audience

Values-driven spending is no longer a niche. It is a fundamental shift in how consumers make decisions.

Values-Driven Shoppers

Millennials and Gen Z aged 22 to 45 who actively seek out brands aligned with their personal values and social causes.

Thoughtful Gift Givers

Shoppers looking for meaningful presents that go beyond the object itself, gifts that reflect the recipient's beliefs and passions.

Lifestyle Navigators

Individuals with dietary or lifestyle preferences (vegan, keto, non-GMO) who need easier and faster product discovery.

Total Addressable Market

$150B+

The ethical consumer market in the US alone exceeds $150 billion, with 73% of millennials willing to pay more for sustainable brands. The affiliate marketing industry generates $8.2 billion annually. Even a small slice of values-driven purchase decisions represents a significant opportunity.

Business Plan

The roadmap to launch

A lean, practical plan to turn conscious consumerism into a sustainable business.

Revenue Model

Affiliate commissions on purchases made through the platform, starting with Amazon affiliate links. As the platform grows, revenue expands to brand partnership fees for featured placement, premium subscription tiers for curated gift guides and advanced filtering, and sponsored cause campaigns from nonprofits and mission-driven brands.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Launch as a web portal with curated Amazon affiliate links across 10 popular cause categories (environmental, cruelty-free, US-made, charitable giving). Build traction through social media content highlighting surprising brand alignments and shareable gift guides. Partner with lifestyle influencers in sustainability, vegan, and conscious living spaces to drive organic growth.

Competitive Advantage

No single platform currently aggregates brand values, cause alignment, lifestyle compatibility, and gift-giving into one consumer-friendly experience. Supportive creates a unique data moat by combining public brand data with community contributions and direct brand partnerships, becoming the definitive resource for conscious consumers.

Estimated Startup Cost

$15,000 - $40,000

Covers initial web development, brand data aggregation, content creation, and early marketing spend. The low-tech MVP with Amazon affiliate links can launch at the lower end of this range.

Year One Milestones

  • Launch web platform with 500+ brands cataloged across 10 cause categories and generate first affiliate revenue
  • Reach 50,000 monthly active users through social media content strategy and influencer partnerships
  • Secure 10 direct brand partnerships for featured placement and begin building the mobile app

Key Risks

  • Data accuracy and keeping brand cause information current as companies change policies or face public controversies
  • Navigating politically polarizing topics without alienating user segments or appearing to endorse specific positions
  • Affiliate revenue margins may be thin at low scale, requiring significant traffic before the business becomes sustainable

Business Plan

The Playbook

A clear path from concept to market dominance in the pre-funded purchasing space.

Revenue Model

Multiple Revenue Streams

Subscription tiers ranging from free (limited watches) to premium (unlimited watches, priority purchasing). Per-transaction convenience fees of 2-5%. Interchange revenue on stored-value accounts. B2B partnerships with retailers who pay for access to a pool of verified, pre-funded buyers.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Start Vertical, Scale Wide

Launch with sneaker drops and gaming console restocks where demand vastly outstrips supply. Partner with 2-3 major retailers to prove the verified-buyer value proposition. Build community through social targeting sneakerheads and gaming enthusiasts, then expand into home goods and collectibles.

Competitive Advantage

Escrow-Backed Trust

Unlike price trackers or wishlist apps, Supercharge holds real funds and executes purchases instantly. The pre-funded model builds trust with retailers who benefit from guaranteed fraud-free transactions. This two-sided value proposition creates a defensible network effect that grows stronger with each new user and partner.

Year One Milestones

The Road to Traction

Key Risks

What Could Go Wrong

  • Retailers may resist third-party purchasing intermediaries or build competing in-house solutions
  • Regulatory complexity around holding customer funds in escrow across multiple states
  • Consumer trust barrier: convincing users to pre-load funds into another digital wallet

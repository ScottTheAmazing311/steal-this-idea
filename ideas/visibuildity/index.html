The Problem

Building a Home
Shouldn't Feel Like a Gamble

Homeowners pour their savings into construction projects, only to be left completely in the dark about what is happening, when it will finish, and whether mistakes are piling up behind the drywall.

  • Timelines That Mean Nothing

    An 8-week estimate turns into 21 weeks. Builders are notorious for missing deadlines with zero explanation. You plan your life around dates that never hold.

  • Radio Silence from Contractors

    You text asking about progress. No response. You call. Voicemail. A simple "we are a few days behind" would change everything, but it never comes.

  • Costly Mistakes Go Unseen

    Wrong fixtures get installed. Unwanted lights go up overnight. By the time you drive by to check, it is too late to course correct without expensive rework.

  • Playing Project Manager for Your Own Home

    You end up coordinating between subcontractors, chasing updates, and managing a build that should be handled for you. It is exhausting.

The Solution

Your Build, Fully Transparent

Visibuildity provides a shared portal where general contractors log progress with checklists, photos, and detailed notes, while homeowners track every milestone from their phone or laptop in real time.

Subcontractors confirm completed tasks via simple text messages. No app downloads. No logins. Just reply "done" and the portal updates instantly.

When your plumber finishes the rough-in, you see a photo before the drywall goes up. When new materials arrive at the site, you verify it is the right toilet before it gets installed. When the timeline shifts, you know exactly why and what it means for your move-in date.

Revenue Model

SaaS subscription sold to builders and general contractors, priced per active project. Small contractors pay a lower monthly fee, while large builders managing hundreds of homes pay premium rates. A secondary revenue stream comes from an aggregated contractor review marketplace powered by platform data.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Partner with 5 to 10 regional home builders to pilot the platform, using their improved homeowner satisfaction scores as case studies. Then expand to individual contractors through a freemium tier and build word-of-mouth via homeowner referrals from people who experienced the platform during their own builds.

Competitive Advantage

Purpose-built for the builder-to-homeowner relationship, not adapted from generic project management. The SMS-based subcontractor interface removes the biggest adoption barrier in construction tech: getting tradespeople to actually use the software. Over time, aggregated data creates a defensible moat via the review marketplace.

Startup Cost Estimate

$75K - $200K

Covers MVP development, SMS integration, initial builder partnerships, and first-year operations.

Year One Milestones

  • Sign 10 paying builder accounts managing at least 50 active projects on the platform
  • Process 5,000 subcontractor text check-ins, proving the SMS workflow at scale
  • Achieve a 40+ Net Promoter Score from homeowners using the portal

Key Risks

  • Contractor adoption: tradespeople may resist any additional reporting requirements, even if it is just replying to a text
  • Builder inertia: large home builders have established workflows and may be slow to change established processes
  • Data accuracy: the platform is only as valuable as the updates that contractors actually submit

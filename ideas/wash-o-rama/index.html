The Problem

Five Minutes of Nothing

Every car wash is the same. You pull in, sit through soap, water, and spinning brushes, then drive away. There is nothing memorable about it, nothing that makes you choose one wash over another.

Car wash operators face a loyalty crisis. With no differentiation, customers default to whatever is cheapest or closest. Margins shrink, churn increases, and there is no reason for anyone to come back.

The Solution

Entertainment on the Track

Choose Your Adventure

Pick from rotating themes: space battles, ocean dives, holiday spectacles, or movie tie-ins before you enter the wash tunnel.

AR on Your Phone

Your smartphone becomes the controller. The AR experience syncs perfectly with the car wash track speed and stages.

LED + Audio

Synchronized LED light shows and surround audio piped through an FM frequency turn the tunnel into a real environment.

Business Plan

The Playbook

Revenue Model

Premium pricing adds $5 to $10 per wash above standard rates. Monthly memberships unlock exclusive experiences and early access to new themes. The licensing model sells the full AR hardware kit and software platform to existing car wash operators for a setup fee plus recurring revenue. Branded experience sponsorships from movie studios and consumer brands add a high-margin revenue stream.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Launch a single flagship location in a high-traffic suburban corridor. Use that location to generate viral social media content (TikTok and Instagram Reels of families reacting to the AR experience). Once the concept is proven and documented, license the technology kit to existing car wash operators who want to differentiate without building from scratch.

Competitive Advantage

Nobody else is combining entertainment with car washing at scale. The controlled environment, with its fixed speed, fixed duration, and captive audience, makes synchronization trivially reliable compared to other location-based AR experiences. Early movers can lock in exclusive franchise territories and content deals with entertainment studios.

Year One Milestones

  • Open one flagship location and achieve 500+ washes per week
  • Launch AR app with 3 themed experiences and hit 10,000 downloads
  • Sign letters of intent with 5 car wash operators for the licensing pilot

Key Risks

  • Hardware installation costs may deter operators from adopting the platform
  • AR adoption requires customers to download an app, creating friction at the tunnel entrance
  • Themed content production costs could be high and require frequent refreshes to sustain interest

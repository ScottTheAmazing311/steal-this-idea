The Solution

Your PM data already has the answers. Let AI read it.

TimelineAI connects to the tools you already use and transforms raw project data into actionable intelligence. Due dates shifting? Tasks piling up? Contributors going quiet? The signals are there. TimelineAI surfaces them before anyone else notices.

Risk Scoring

Every project gets a dynamic risk score updated in real time based on task velocity, due date changes, and team activity patterns.

Timeline Intelligence

Compares new deadlines against your historical data and flags timelines that are too ambitious before your team commits to them.

Bottleneck Detection

Identifies which teams or workflows consistently cause delays and recommends resource shifts to keep delivery on track.

Market Opportunity

A $7B market with zero AI-native players

The global project management software market is valued at roughly $7 billion and growing over 10% annually. Despite AI flooding every other software category, there is no dominant AI-native solution in project management. That gap is the opportunity.

Every company runs projects. Almost none predict risk.

From 10-person startups to Fortune 500 enterprises, everyone manages projects. Yet the tools they use simply track tasks without learning from patterns. TimelineAI turns passive tracking into active intelligence.

Business Plan

The blueprint to build this

TimelineAI: Business Plan

AI-powered project risk intelligence for teams that ship

Revenue Model

SaaS subscription with per-seat pricing. Free tier for small teams (up to 10 users). Pro at $15/user/month for risk scoring and timeline intelligence. Enterprise at $35/user/month with custom ML models, SSO, and dedicated support. Additional revenue from integration marketplace and onboarding services.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Launch as a plug-in integration for Asana and Jira to tap into their existing user bases. Offer a free tier to attract small teams, then convert to paid plans as organizations scale. Target product-led growth through content marketing, PM community partnerships, and case studies from early adopters.

Competitive Advantage

No existing PM tool offers true predictive intelligence. Asana, Monday, and Jira track tasks but do not learn from patterns. Unlike AI sales tools where data is dirty and outcomes are client-dependent, project management data is clean and internally controlled, making ML predictions far more reliable. TimelineAI is first to market in a category that should already exist.

Year One Milestones
  • Ship Asana and Jira integrations with risk scoring and timeline intelligence for 500 beta users
  • Reach $250K ARR with 50 paying teams across Pro and Enterprise tiers
  • Publish three case studies demonstrating measurable improvement in on-time project delivery rates
Startup Cost Estimate
$150K - $350K to MVP

Covers engineering team, cloud infrastructure (AWS/GCP for ML workloads), and initial go-to-market spend.

Key Risks
  • Platform dependency: Reliance on Asana/Jira APIs means changes to those platforms could break core functionality
  • Data cold start: New customers may not have enough historical project data for accurate ML predictions initially
  • Incumbents adding AI: Asana, Monday, or Jira could build similar features natively, shrinking the addressable market

SecuriBot

// your neighborhood never sleeps

An autonomous patrol robot equipped with 360-degree cameras and AI-powered threat detection. Shared among neighbors, it roams the streets at night, streaming live footage and deterring crime simply by being visible.

// Problem

Static cameras have blind spots. Criminals know it.

Break-ins and petty crime remain common even in safe-looking neighborhoods. Ring doorbells and static cameras cover fixed angles that are easy to avoid. They require homeowners to actively monitor alerts. Most neighborhoods have no coordinated security presence at all.

// Solution

A moving, watching, thinking patrol.

SecuriBot is a Roomba-style outdoor robot that autonomously patrols your neighborhood on a nightly schedule. Its 360-degree camera feeds live to a professional monitoring center, and AI flags suspicious activity in real time.

Unpredictable patrol routes make it impossible for criminals to time their approach
Visible physical presence acts as a powerful deterrent that static cameras cannot match
Shared cost model splits the investment across 10 to 15 households
// Capabilities

Built for the real world.

Every component of SecuriBot is engineered for outdoor durability, intelligent navigation, and community-level awareness.

AI Path Planning

Autonomous nightly patrol routes optimized by machine learning. The bot adapts to terrain, avoids obstacles, and randomizes its path so it is never predictable.

360-Degree Night Vision

Live-streaming cameras with infrared night vision and license plate recognition. Every angle covered, every moment recorded and backed up to the cloud.

Self-Charging Dock

Deploys at dusk, returns at dawn. The home-base dock charges the unit fully, uploads footage, and runs diagnostic checks for the next patrol cycle.

Community Alerts

When SecuriBot detects suspicious activity, every participating household gets an instant notification. A shared awareness network that keeps the whole neighborhood informed.

// Business Plan

From prototype to patrol.

Revenue Model

Hardware sales at approximately $1,000 per unit, split among 10 to 15 participating households. Optional professional monitoring subscription at $20 per month per community. Premium AI analytics and incident reporting tiers available for larger communities.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Partner directly with HOA management companies to bundle SecuriBot into community safety packages. Run pilot programs in 10 neighborhoods across different regions and publish crime-reduction data to drive word-of-mouth referrals and earn media coverage.

Competitive Advantage

Unlike static Ring doorbells and security cameras, SecuriBot is mobile and unpredictable, covering entire neighborhoods rather than single doorsteps. Its shared-cost model makes professional-grade security accessible at a fraction of the price of hiring a human patrol service.

Year One Milestones

  • Complete hardware prototype and secure FCC certification for wireless components
  • Deploy pilot units in 10 HOA communities and collect 6 months of performance data
  • Reach 100 communities and establish a professional monitoring operations center

Startup Cost Estimate

Estimated investment through prototype and first production batch:

$500,000 to $1,500,000

Key Risks

  • Local regulations and privacy laws may restrict autonomous surveillance robots on public or shared streets
  • Vandalism or theft of the robot itself, despite onboard cameras and live monitoring
  • Weather durability and reliability concerns for year-round outdoor operation in all climates

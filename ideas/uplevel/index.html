The Solution

One Box. Five Best.
Your Call.

UpLevel is the Warby Parker model, applied to everything. Our editorial team tests, ranks, and curates the five best products in each category. We ship them to your door. You try them, keep your favorite, and send back the rest with a prepaid label.

  1. Pick Your Category

    Wallets, over-ear headphones, sunglasses, key organizers, and more. Choose what you need to upgrade.

  2. Receive Your Box

    Five expert-curated products arrive at your door. Each one is a top performer in its category, chosen by our editorial panel.

  3. Try Them All

    Spend up to a week testing every option in your real life. No rush, no pressure, no store lighting tricks.

  4. Keep the One You Love

    Pay only for the product you keep. Pack the rest in the prepaid return box and drop it off. Done.

Who Is the UpLevel Customer?

Millennial and Gen-Z consumers who value quality but hate wading through infinite options. They watch YouTube reviews, read Reddit threads, and still feel unsure. They want someone they trust to narrow the field so they can make the final call themselves. Secondary audience: gift-givers looking for something more thoughtful than a gift card, more practical than a novelty item.

Business Plan

The Road to Revenue

A clear, sustainable path from first box to scaled marketplace.

Revenue Model

  • Product markup on items customers keep (15-25% margin through wholesale purchasing)
  • Flat $15 trial fee per box covering curation, shipping, and handling
  • Premium membership with recurring category drops at discounted rates

Go-to-Market

Launch with three hero categories where analysis paralysis peaks: over-ear headphones, slim wallets, and sunglasses. Partner with micro-influencers in the EDC (everyday carry) and tech review communities for authentic promotion. Offer a first-box-free trial (pay only for what you keep) to drive adoption.

Competitive Advantage

Unlike Amazon, where you sift through thousands of listings and contradictory reviews, UpLevel does the research with a trusted editorial team. Unlike Warby Parker, UpLevel is not limited to one product vertical. The try-before-you-commit model drastically reduces buyer's remorse and builds lasting customer trust.

Year One Milestones

  • Launch three product categories and ship 10,000 curated boxes
  • Achieve a 60%+ keep rate per box to validate unit economics
  • Build a membership base of 2,000 recurring subscribers

Key Risks

  • High return logistics costs if most users send back four out of five items without purchasing
  • Difficulty negotiating wholesale terms with premium brands before achieving scale
  • Customer churn if people use the service to identify products, then buy cheaper elsewhere

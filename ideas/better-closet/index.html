The Solution

Tag it. Scan it. Forget about it.

Better Closet pairs penny-sized RFID stickers with three compact scanners to give you total visibility into your wardrobe without changing how you live.

1

Tag your wardrobe

Stick a tiny, washable RFID tag on each garment. Takes about an hour for your whole closet. Snap a photo so the app knows what each item looks like.

2

Place three scanners

Mount compact RFID scanners at your closet, hamper, and washer. They automatically detect tagged garments that pass nearby.

3

Live your life

The app handles everything else. It tracks wear patterns, sends laundry reminders, suggests outfits based on weather, and flags items you should sell or donate.

> 3 scanners. 100 tags. One app.
> Your entire wardrobe, mapped.

Know when your favorites are piling up in the hamper. Get a nudge before you run out of clean clothes you actually want to wear.

See exactly where every item is at any moment: hanging in the closet, sitting in the hamper, or tumbling in the wash. No guesswork.

Spring Cleaning Mode

AI analyzes your wear patterns and surfaces items you never touch. See estimated resale value through integrations with ThredUp and Poshmark.

Weather-Aware Suggestions

Rain on Thursday? All your hoodies in the hamper? Better Closet connects to weather data and flags conflicts before you get caught off guard.

Business Plan

From concept to closet

Better Closet // Business Overview

Confidential Draft
Revenue Model
Hardware starter kit sold at $99 to $149 (three RFID scanners plus 100 washable tags). Optional monthly subscription at $5 to $9/month for premium features: outfit planning, weather integration, resale marketplace connections, and AI wardrobe analytics. Additional affiliate revenue from recommending items on ThredUp and Poshmark.
Go-to-Market
Launch a Kickstarter campaign targeting minimalism, sustainability, and smart-home communities. Seed early adopters through lifestyle and home-organization influencers on Instagram and TikTok. Partner with one mid-tier clothing brand to pre-tag garments as a proof-of-concept co-marketing play.
Competitive Advantage
No competitor offers a full-loop garment tracking system from closet to hamper to wash and back. The combination of affordable RFID hardware with an intelligent software layer creates a data flywheel that improves with every wear cycle. Washable adhesive RFID tags remove the biggest friction point of inventory-based closet systems.
Year One Milestones
  • Ship 5,000 hardware starter kits through a successful crowdfunding campaign
  • Reach 2,000 monthly active subscribers on the premium app tier
  • Secure one brand partnership for pre-tagged clothing integration
Startup Cost
$75K to $200K
Key Risks
  • Consumer friction during initial setup: tagging and scanning every garment requires real effort up front
  • RFID tag durability through repeated wash and dry cycles may frustrate users if tags fail
  • Competing with simpler low-tech habits: many people tolerate closet chaos rather than adopting new systems

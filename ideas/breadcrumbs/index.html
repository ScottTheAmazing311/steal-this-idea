BreadCrumbs

Drop a pin. Share the find.

A social geotagging app that lets you leave location-based tips, discoveries, and deals for others to find. Think of it as a crowdsourced layer of local knowledge on top of the real world.

Start Dropping Crumbs See How It Works

The Problem

The best local finds stay hidden

Free air at a gas station. A secret lookout with a perfect view. A flash sale on Paw Patrol toys at Walmart. These discoveries live and die in private texts, never reaching the people who need them most.

  • Local gems are invisible

    The coolest spots, free perks, and secret finds never make it to Yelp or Google Maps. They exist only in word of mouth.

  • Flash deals vanish instantly

    Pop-up sales, the Red Bull truck, clearance racks: by the time you hear about them, they are already gone.

  • No tool for informal tips

    There is a whole layer of useful, casual, hyper-local info that sits between "Yelp review" and "text your friend." Nothing serves that space.

The Solution

Leave a trail for everyone

BreadCrumbs puts a living, breathing layer of community knowledge on top of your map. Snap, tag, and share. It is that simple.

1

Find something worth sharing

Free air at the gas station, an amazing lookout, a killer clearance sale, a pop-up event. Anything that would make someone's day a little better.

2

Drop a breadcrumb

Snap a photo, tag it with a category, and choose if it is permanent or temporary. Post it right from the app, or just throw #breadcrumbs on your Instagram story.

3

The community benefits

Anyone nearby gets notified. Trending breadcrumbs grow on the map. Over time, every neighborhood fills with useful, fun, and surprising discoveries.

Features

Built for the curious

Every feature is designed to make sharing and discovering local knowledge as frictionless as possible.

Geo-tagged Photo Pins

Every breadcrumb lives on the map with a photo, a location, and a category. Mark it permanent for evergreen tips, or time-limited for flash deals and pop-ups.

Trending Smart Map

Breadcrumbs that get engagement grow bigger on the map. You can see at a glance what is hot in any neighborhood, right now.

Instagram Integration

Just post to your story with #breadcrumbs and the app pulls it in automatically. No extra steps, no separate login. Share how you already share.

Proximity Alerts

Walk into a breadcrumb-rich zone and get a gentle nudge. "There are 12 crumbs nearby." Open the app and see what your neighbors have found.

Market Opportunity

A map for the rest of us

Urban millennials and Gen Z (ages 18 to 35) already live on location-based apps like Waze, Yelp, and Instagram. BreadCrumbs captures the informal, ephemeral layer of local knowledge that no review platform covers.

Business Plan

The path to crumb domination

Revenue Model

Sponsored breadcrumbs from local businesses and national brands. In-app display advertising. Premium tier for power users with advanced filtering, saved collections, and early notifications on nearby drops.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Launch on a single college campus with 50 seed users who pre-populate the map. Grow virally through Instagram hashtag sharing and word of mouth. Expand city by city, targeting college towns and urban neighborhoods with high foot traffic.

Competitive Advantage

Unlike Yelp or Google Maps, BreadCrumbs captures the informal, ephemeral layer that no review platform covers: flash sales, free perks, scenic lookouts, pop-up events. Instagram integration lowers the barrier to contribution far below any competitor.

Key Risks

  • Cold start problem: the map feels empty until a critical mass of users contribute content in each area
  • Content moderation challenges with user-generated geo-tagged material that could include spam or inaccurate tips
  • Platform dependency on Instagram API access, which could be restricted or deprecated

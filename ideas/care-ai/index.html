Business Plan

Built to scale with every scan.

Revenue Model

Freemium Subscription

Free tier includes basic skin scoring and weekly scans. Premium tier at $9.99/month (or $59.99/year) unlocks daily tracking, detailed analytics, product correlations, and personalized routine suggestions. Affiliate partnerships with skincare brands add a secondary revenue stream.

Go-to-Market

Social-First Launch

Launch a free AI skin scoring tool designed for virality on TikTok and Instagram through influencer partnerships and shareable before/after content. Target existing skincare communities on Reddit and YouTube. Convert free users to premium through gated analytics and personalized insights.

Competitive Advantage

Data That Deepens Over Time

Unlike apps that offer tips or routines, CareAI provides objective, data-driven skin analysis. The longer users stay, the more personalized their experience becomes. This creates a strong retention loop and a defensible dataset of skin-product correlations that competitors cannot replicate easily.

Year One Milestones
  • Launch MVP on iOS with core AI scanning, scoring, and product logging within 4 months
  • Reach 100,000 free users and 5,000 premium subscribers within 9 months through influencer-led campaigns
  • Secure 3 to 5 affiliate partnerships with skincare brands and launch the recommendation engine by month 12
Key Risks
  • Regulatory challenges around health or dermatological claims, requiring careful positioning as a wellness product
  • Accuracy of phone camera-based analysis across different lighting, devices, and skin tones may undermine trust
  • User retention may suffer if scores feel too slow to change or do not align with self-perception

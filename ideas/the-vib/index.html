The Solution

A citywide network of premium restrooms, accessible only to members.

The V.I.B. deploys climate-controlled, regularly sanitized portable restrooms that play ambient music. Badge-in access limits traffic and creates accountability, keeping every unit immaculate. An app shows real-time availability and locations near you. Starting at just $9.99 per month.

Ambient Experience

Climate control, soft lighting, and curated music transform a routine stop into something almost pleasant.

Badge-In Access

Key fob or badge entry limits traffic, creates accountability, and ensures every unit stays pristine for every visit.

Real-Time App

Find the nearest V.I.B., check availability, and navigate there instantly. Never wonder or worry again.

Market Opportunity

Premium restrooms for those who refuse to settle.

Target Market

  • Urban professionals in office parks with inadequate restroom ratios
  • Frequent event and concert attendees tired of standard porta-potties
  • Gig economy workers, including delivery drivers and rideshare operators, who need reliable access
  • Outdoor enthusiasts and park visitors
  • Event organizers seeking a premium restroom option for their venues

The Opportunity

The global portable restroom market exceeds $7 billion with steady year-over-year growth. Premium restroom rentals for events already command 3 to 5x standard pricing, proving willingness to pay for quality.

$60M+ ARR

The V.I.B. Business Plan

Confidential / For Review Only

Revenue Model

  • Individual subscriptions at $9.99/month
  • Corporate office park plans at $500 to $2,000/month
  • Event rental packages at premium rates
  • Per-use swipe option at $1 to $2 for non-subscribers
  • Hardware revenue from selling/leasing premium units to venue operators

Go-to-Market Strategy

  • Launch with 20 to 30 units in a single city at high-traffic office parks and outdoor venues
  • Secure corporate partnerships first for guaranteed recurring revenue
  • Build consumer base through events and word-of-mouth
  • Expand city-by-city once unit economics are proven

Competitive Advantage

The portable restroom industry has had zero consumer-facing innovation. Honey Bucket and competitors compete on price for a commodity product. The V.I.B. creates an entirely new premium tier with recurring revenue, brand loyalty, and network effects. Badge access solves the vandalism and overcrowding problems that make current porta-potties terrible. There is no incumbent to displace; only a vacuum to fill.

Year One Milestones

  • Deploy 30 premium units across one metro area with 2,000+ active subscribers
  • Sign 5 corporate office park partnerships for guaranteed recurring revenue
  • Prove unit economics: each unit generating 2x its monthly maintenance cost

Startup Cost

Premium unit manufacturing, placement logistics, app development, and initial maintenance operations.

$150,000 to $400,000

Key Risks

  • High upfront capital required to achieve sufficient network density
  • Vandalism and misuse despite badge system could erode the premium experience
  • Consumer willingness to pay monthly for restroom access is unproven in the U.S. market

Rentastic

Your stuff, their adventure.

Turn your idle gear into vacation gold. Rentastic connects gear owners with vacation rental hosts so guests get e-bikes, golf clubs, game consoles, and more delivered right to their door. Everyone wins: hosts stand out, owners earn, travelers explore.

The Problem

Every listing looks
exactly the same.

Vacation rentals compete on price alone, while travelers crave unique experiences and gear owners watch their equipment gather dust in the garage.

Hosts can't differentiate

With thousands of listings in popular destinations, vacation rentals blend together. Hosts compete on price in a race to the bottom.

Travelers settle for less

Guests want to explore with e-bikes, hit the trails, or have game night. But renting gear from shops is overpriced and inconvenient.

Gear owners lose money

Golf clubs, kayaks, and game consoles sit idle for months. Owners have no easy way to monetize equipment they rarely use.

The Solution

A three-sided marketplace
where everyone wins.

Rentastic connects gear owners, vacation rental hosts, and travelers into one seamless loop of value.

1

Owners list their gear

Golf clubs, e-bikes, paddleboards, gaming consoles, board game collections. If you own it and don't use it every day, list it on Rentastic.

2

Hosts add experience packages

Vacation rental hosts browse nearby gear and build curated add-on packages. "Adventure Pack" with e-bikes and a kayak? Done.

3

Guests book and enjoy

Travelers see upgraded listings with real perks, book the add-ons, and find everything ready at their rental. No shops, no hassle.

Everything is insured

Rentastic covers every item with full protection. Owners rent with confidence; guests use gear worry-free.

Features

Built for the sharing economy,
perfected for vacation rentals.

Seamless Platform Integration

Plug directly into Airbnb and VRBO listings. Gear add-ons appear as booking upgrades, keeping the experience smooth for guests and hosts alike.

Full Insurance Coverage

Every item rented through Rentastic is protected with comprehensive damage coverage. Owners and guests both pay a small premium for total peace of mind.

Smart Geo Matching

Our algorithm automatically pairs nearby gear owners with vacation rental hosts. The closer the gear, the easier the logistics for everyone involved.

Curated Experience Packages

Bundle bikes, boards, and board games into themed packages. "Family Fun," "Adventure Seeker," "Cozy Night In": give guests a reason to pick your listing.

Business Plan

The roadmap to launch.

A clear, actionable plan to turn this concept into a real company.

Revenue Model

Rentastic takes a percentage cut from each gear rental transaction, charged to both the host and the gear owner. Additional revenue streams include optional insurance premiums and featured listing placements for hosts who want to spotlight their add-on packages.

Go-to-Market

Launch in three high-density vacation rental markets: Park City, Destin, and Lake Tahoe. Partner directly with local Airbnb host groups and recreational equipment retailers to seed both sides of the marketplace from day one.

Competitive Advantage

Rentastic operates at the intersection of the sharing economy and vacation rentals, a gap no major player fills. By embedding directly into existing booking flows, it creates strong network effects: more gear means better listings, which means more guests, which attracts more gear owners.

Year One Milestones

  • Launch in 3 pilot vacation markets with 500+ active gear listings
  • Achieve 2,000 completed gear rentals with a sub-3% damage claim rate
  • Secure API partnership or integration approval with at least one major booking platform

Key Risks

  • Gear damage or theft could erode owner trust and increase insurance costs significantly
  • Airbnb or VRBO could build native add-on features, cutting out the middleman
  • Cold-start problem: need both gear supply and host demand in the same geographic area

Estimated Startup Cost

$150K - $300K

Covers MVP development, initial insurance partnerships, pilot market launch costs, and early marketing spend across three target markets.

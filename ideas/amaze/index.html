AMAZE

The Year-Round Indoor Maze Experience

A permanent, immersive, story-driven maze entertainment venue. Modular walls, rotating themes, multi-level exploration, and frictionless tap-to-pay entry. The thrill of a corn maze, every single day of the year.

Enter the Maze

The Problem

Group Entertainment Is Stale

Adults are starving for fresh, affordable activities. The options right now are bowling, trampoline parks, and laser tag. Nothing truly new. Nothing that makes you say "I have to go back."

Seasonal Only

Corn mazes run for a few weeks each fall. The rest of the year, that itch goes unscratched.

Escape Rooms Are Expensive

$30+ per person, one group at a time, 20-minute resets between sessions. The model has a hard ceiling.

Nothing Feels New

People are desperate for something original. Every entertainment venue feels like a copy of a copy.

The Solution

Walk In. Get Lost. Come Back.

Amaze transforms empty warehouses into sprawling, immersive indoor mazes. Walls move on a modular grid, so every month brings a new layout. Themes rotate quarterly: one month it is Dracula's Castle with fog machines and red lighting, the next you are navigating a sci-fi space station.

Dozens of groups explore simultaneously. No resets. No reservations. Tap your card and you are in. Under $10 a person for hours of exploration, puzzle-solving, and genuine adventure.

Key Features

Built to Amaze

Modular Walls

Every wall sits on a grid system. Reconfigure monthly for new layouts. Full theme overhauls each quarter keep veterans coming back for more.

Multi-Dimensional Paths

Not just left and right. Crawl through tunnels, climb stairs, discover second stories. Hidden secret rooms with free snacks reward the most dedicated explorers.

Story-Driven Puzzles

RFID key cards unlock color-coded doors. Map rubbings reveal hidden paths. Collectible checkpoints let you save progress and return next time to push further.

Tap and Enter

No reservation. No training session. No awkward lobby wait. Tap your card at the door and your timer starts. Walk in, get lost, find your way out.

The Business Plan

How Amaze Makes Money

Revenue Model

Pay-per-entry pricing under $10/person for a 1-2 hour experience. Additional revenue streams include themed concessions (maze-shaped waffles, twisted churros), branded merchandise, premium events (zombie chase nights, 48-hour extreme mazes), and private group bookings for corporate teams and parties.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Launch a single flagship location by leasing an empty warehouse in a high-traffic retail corridor. Start with two difficulty tiers and one theme. Validate demand with social media content, then scale to monthly rotations. Use Minecraft-style virtual prototyping to test maze designs before physical construction.

Competitive Advantage

No reset time between groups. Dozens of simultaneous visitors. A fraction of the cost of escape rooms. Year-round operation where corn mazes are seasonal. The modular wall system enables constant freshness that static venues simply cannot replicate.

Startup Cost Estimate

Warehouse lease, modular wall system fabrication, initial theme buildout, marketing, and first quarter operations.

$75K - $150K

Year One Milestones

  • Open flagship warehouse location and achieve 10,000 visitors in the first quarter
  • Launch monthly wall reconfiguration cycle and quarterly theme rotations to drive repeat visits
  • Introduce premium events and hit $500K in annual revenue

Key Risks

  • High upfront capital for warehouse lease, modular wall systems, and buildout before revenue begins
  • Unproven year-round demand for maze experiences outside the seasonal corn maze window
  • Theme and layout freshness requires ongoing creative investment to prevent visitor fatigue

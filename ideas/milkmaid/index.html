Features

Built for safety. Designed for families.

Rigorous Supplier Vetting

Every supplier completes health screenings, background checks, and dietary verification before listing on the platform.

Smart Matching Engine

Filter suppliers by dietary preferences, location, availability, and health certifications to find your perfect match.

Market Opportunity

A massive, underserved need.

Target Market

New mothers and families in the U.S. who need breast milk but face supply, dietary, or lifestyle barriers. This includes NICU families, adoptive parents, and surrogacy arrangements.

$270M+
estimated annual addressable market in the U.S.

Why Now

The cultural shift toward normalizing breastfeeding conversations, combined with the gig economy mindset, creates a unique window. Mothers are more open to peer-to-peer solutions, and suppliers see an opportunity to earn while helping other families. The technology for cold-chain logistics and identity verification has never been more accessible.

Business Plan

The roadmap to launch.

MilkMaid Business Plan

A two-sided marketplace for safe, vetted breast milk

Revenue Model

Annual membership fee ($99/year for buyers, free for sellers) plus a 15% transaction fee on each sale. Premium tier offers priority matching, expedited shipping, and detailed supplier health reports for $29/month.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Launch in 3 to 5 metro areas with high birth rates and strong parenting communities: Austin, Denver, and Portland. Partner with lactation consultants, doulas, and NICU social workers for referrals. Build trust through a clinical advisory board and transparent safety standards.

Competitive Advantage

MilkMaid is the only platform that combines clinical-grade supplier vetting with the convenience of a modern marketplace. Milk banks serve hospitals first and are chronically undersupplied. Facebook groups offer no safety guarantees. MilkMaid fills the gap with trust, transparency, and technology.

Year One Milestones

  • Onboard 500 vetted suppliers and 1,500 active buyers across 5 launch markets
  • Establish clinical advisory board and obtain any required state-level certifications
  • Reach $50K monthly gross merchandise volume with a 4.5+ star average supplier rating

Startup Cost Estimate

Platform development, legal and regulatory counsel, initial supplier screening infrastructure, and cold-chain shipping partnerships.

$150K to $350K to MVP

Key Risks

  • Regulatory hurdles: breast milk sales occupy a legal gray area in many states, requiring careful navigation of FDA and state health department guidelines
  • Trust and safety liability: any contamination incident could be catastrophic for brand reputation and could trigger lawsuits
  • Marketplace leakage: buyers and sellers may connect on-platform and then transact off-platform to avoid fees

The Problem

Sitting Is the New Smoking

Remote work freed us from the commute, but chained us to the chair. Under-desk pedals exist, but nobody sticks with them.

No Motivation to Keep Going

You buy an under-desk exerciser. You use it for a week. Then it collects dust. The problem is not the hardware. The problem is that there is zero reason to keep pedaling once the novelty wears off.

Fitness trackers give you numbers, but numbers alone do not create habit loops. You need something that makes you care, something that makes you feel a tiny pang of guilt when you stop.

The Solution

Your Pet Needs You to Pedal

CyclePet turns desk exercise into a charming game you never want to stop playing.

A Bluetooth pedal device syncs to a Chrome extension with a virtual pet whose whole world depends on your movement. Pedal and the sun shines. Stop and the clouds roll in.

Pedal = Sunshine

Your movement powers the sun in your pet's world. The faster you go, the brighter it shines and the happier your pet becomes.

Stop = Storm Clouds

Take a break and clouds gather. Rain starts. Your pet looks at you with big sad eyes. You will start pedaling again.

Consistency = Growth

Keep pedaling daily and your pet's garden grows. Unlock new pets, new environments, and seasonal events throughout the year.

Key Features

Everything You Need to Stay Moving

From the hardware under your desk to the virtual world on your screen, CyclePet makes the whole day more active and a lot more fun.

1

Bluetooth Pedal Sync

A whisper-quiet under-desk elliptical with built-in Bluetooth that streams your pedal cadence and speed directly to your browser in real time.

2

Virtual Pet World

A Tamagotchi-style Chrome extension where pedaling controls weather, food growth, and your pet's mood. Choose from 50+ collectible pets.

3

Streaks and Unlockables

Daily streaks, seasonal environments, and themed events keep the experience fresh. The longer your streak, the rarer the rewards.

4

Earned Break Time

Productivity mode converts your pedal minutes into guilt-free screen break credits. Pedal for 30 minutes, earn 5 minutes of YouTube time.

Business Plan

How CyclePet Becomes a Business

A clear path from Kickstarter launch to sustainable growth, combining hardware sales with recurring digital revenue.

Revenue Model

Hardware sales of the branded CyclePet pedal device at $149 to $199 per unit, plus a freemium Chrome extension. Premium pet packs, environments, and seasonal content available through a $4.99/month subscription or $2.99 one-time expansion packs.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Launch on Kickstarter with a playful campaign video showing office workers bonding over their virtual pets. Seed early units to productivity YouTubers and work-from-home influencers. Follow up with DTC sales and a free-tier Chrome extension to build viral word of mouth.

Competitive Advantage

Existing under-desk exercisers are hardware-only products with no engagement layer. CyclePet is the first to combine casual desk fitness with a charming virtual pet game loop, creating an emotional reason to keep pedaling that pure fitness tracking cannot match.

Year One Milestones

  • Launch Kickstarter campaign and ship 5,000 units to early backers within 6 months
  • Release Chrome extension with 10 unlockable pets and 4 seasonal environments
  • Reach 15,000 active daily users and establish partnerships with 3 corporate wellness programs

Key Risks

  • Hardware manufacturing and logistics are capital-intensive and introduce supply chain risk for a startup
  • User novelty may wear off after the initial excitement, requiring constant content updates to retain engagement
  • Larger fitness brands like Peloton or Cubii could replicate the gamification layer and bundle it with superior hardware

The Microwave of Dishwashing

Dishly

One dish. Ten seconds. Spotless.

A compact countertop appliance that washes a single dish, cup, or utensil in 10 seconds flat. No more waiting for a full load. No more crusty, forgotten dishes. Just clean, instantly.

The Problem

Dishwashers Were Built for a Different Era

The dishwasher was designed in the 1950s for large families producing mountains of dishes. Today, 37 million Americans live alone. Couples, students, and office workers generate one or two dirty items at a time. The result? Dishes pile up, food crusts onto bowls, and you end up pre-rinsing everything before loading a machine that takes an hour to run half-empty.

The Solution

Wash It Now.
Not Later.

Dishly reimagines dishwashing from the ground up. One item at a time, cleaned and sanitized in seconds. It is the microwave equivalent that the dishwasher never had.

  • HIGH-PRESSURE JETS

    Aerated water blasts away food particles with precision, targeting every surface of the dish in seconds.

  • UV STERILIZATION

    Hospital-grade UV light kills 99.9% of bacteria after every wash, no harsh chemicals required.

  • COMPRESSED-AIR DRY

    Instant forced-air drying means your dish comes out ready to use, not dripping wet.

  • WATER RECYCLING

    Built-in filtration cleans and recirculates water, so you can run dozens of cycles without refilling.

Key Features

Engineered for the
Way You Actually Live

01

10-Second Wash Cycle

Pressurized aerated water jets strip food residue, rinse, and prep the dish for sterilization in a single rapid burst. Put your cup in, hold down the lid, and it is done before you finish your next thought.

02

UV + Compressed Air

UV-C light sterilizes every surface while compressed air flash-dries the item. Your dish comes out clean, dry, and ready to use immediately. No towel needed.

03

Self-Contained Water System

Advanced filtration recycles water between cycles. No plumbing hookup needed. Just plug it in, fill the reservoir, and go. Perfect for dorms, offices, and small apartments.

04

Soap Tablet System

Drop in a proprietary cleaning tablet that lasts for dozens of washes. No measuring, no mess, no bottles cluttering the counter. Subscribe for automatic refills.

Business Plan

The Roadmap to
a New Category

REVENUE MODEL

Hardware sales at $189-$240 per unit for the consumer model, with premium pricing for commercial/office units. Recurring revenue from proprietary soap tablets sold via subscription (razor-blade model). Optional custom-designed dishware sets sold as accessories create ecosystem lock-in and maximize cleaning performance.

GO-TO-MARKET

Launch through a Kickstarter campaign targeting minimalists, tiny-home enthusiasts, and productivity-obsessed office managers. Fuel viral growth with 10-second demo videos on TikTok and Instagram. Secure early B2B pilots with co-working chains and university housing departments for bulk orders.

COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE

Dishly creates an entirely new product category with no direct competitors. Traditional dishwashers cannot serve the single-item use case. The combination of proprietary cleaning tablets, optional custom dishware, and a self-contained water system creates a defensible ecosystem. First-mover advantage in the rapid single-item wash space is significant.

KEY RISKS
  • Hardware development and manufacturing complexity could delay time to market and inflate costs beyond initial projections
  • Consumer skepticism that a 10-second cycle can truly sanitize dishes to the standard they expect from traditional methods
  • Established appliance giants like Samsung, Bosch, or LG could enter the category with greater manufacturing resources and distribution networks

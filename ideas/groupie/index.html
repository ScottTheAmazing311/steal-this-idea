The Problem

Errands are lonely. Tasks pile up. Friends drift apart.

Everyone has a never-ending list of errands and chores they dread doing alone: flu shots, oil changes, grocery runs, IKEA trips. These tasks pile up because they feel tedious in isolation.

  • Your friends are doing the same errands separately, across town, on different days
  • Texting "anyone want to come to Target?" feels awkward and often goes unanswered
  • You forget a friend is nearby who could grab something for you on their way home
The Solution

Your friend group, synchronized.

Groupie lets your friend group share tasks and automatically matches people with overlapping errands or nearby locations. No more solo chores, no more missed opportunities to hang out.

Key Features

Everything your friend group needs

Four core features that make coordinating with friends feel effortless.

Task Matching

Automatically pairs friends with overlapping errands and suggests group outings so no one goes alone.

Location Alerts

Get notified when a friend is near a store you need something from. One tap to request a pickup.

Favor Coins

A built-in economy that tracks who helped whom, keeping the give-and-take balanced across your group.

Instant Payments

One-tap Venmo and Apple Pay integration for seamless reimbursements. Set spending thresholds per friend.

Business Plan

The roadmap to launch

Groupie Business Plan

A social coordination app for friend groups

Revenue Model

Freemium model with a free tier for basic task sharing and group coordination. Premium tier at $4.99/month unlocks smart location-based notifications, Venmo and Apple Pay integration, spending thresholds, and favor analytics. Additional revenue comes from local business partnerships that offer deals when groups visit together.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Launch as a free app targeting college towns and dense suburban friend groups through TikTok and Instagram influencer campaigns. Seed initial groups by partnering with local businesses (coffee shops, gyms) who offer group discounts. Focus on one metro area first to build density, then expand city by city.

Competitive Advantage

Unlike generic group chats or calendar apps, Groupie is purpose-built for real-world coordination among close friend groups. The combination of task matching, location awareness, and a favor economy creates a unique social layer that no existing app replicates. Network effects within friend groups drive organic retention and make switching costly.

Key Risks
  • Network effects require a critical mass of friends on the platform before it becomes useful, creating a cold-start problem
  • Users may default to existing group chats (iMessage, WhatsApp) for the same coordination, limiting switching incentives
  • Location tracking features may raise privacy concerns and require careful opt-in design to avoid feeling invasive
